Research profile / 2026

Models are useful only when their assumptions remain visible.

My current work connects human movement, musculoskeletal simulation and marine engineering. I focus on traceable modelling, sensitivity analysis and critical validation rather than treating software output as evidence by itself.

Methodological principles

A researcher-style workflow should make uncertainty inspectable, not hide it behind polished figures.

01

Define

State the physical question, coordinate systems, units and experimental limits.

02

Reconstruct

Use markers and external loads to reconstruct movement and joint-level quantities.

03

Stress-test

Compare scenarios and numerical assumptions to identify fragile conclusions.

04

Interpret

Separate observations, model-derived results and hypotheses for future validation.
