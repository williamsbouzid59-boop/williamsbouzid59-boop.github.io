Current research / 04

MODEL ESTIMATE VALIDATE ITERATE
Erasmus+ research internship · DITEN, University of Genoa

Applied modelling and validation at the intersection of human movement and marine environments.

My final-year internship studies Motion Induced Interruptions and postural stability in marine environments. The work combines OpenSim musculoskeletal modelling, SCONE simulations, biomechanical data processing, inverse kinematics, inverse dynamics, static optimisation, seakeeping concepts and critical model validation. Technical details remain selective while the project is ongoing.
