How projects are evaluated

The same critical framework is applied across domains.

QUESTION

What is being estimated?

The project begins with a measurable engineering question.

DATA

What supports the result?

Inputs, units, preprocessing and uncertainty are made explicit.

METHOD

Why this model?

Method selection is justified against alternatives and assumptions.

LIMITS

What remains uncertain?

Failure cases and sensitivity are part of the final result.
